Common questions

Where is my data stored?
Your cards and decks are stored locally on your device.

Text-to-speech isn’t speaking.
Check your device volume and Silent Mode, and ensure the selected language voice is available in iOS Settings.

How do I reset a learning run?
Use Shuffle to bring all cards back and start a fresh run.
